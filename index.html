The Problem

Focus is broken by design.

Modern environments are engineered for distraction. Students and professionals lose hours daily without ever realizing it.

⏱
6 min
Average focus duration before distraction
The average person can only maintain deep focus for about 6 minutes before their attention drifts — a figure that has only worsened with smartphone use.
📉
65%
Students report chronic distraction during study
Nearly two-thirds of students say distraction is their number one obstacle to academic performance — yet they have no real-time tool to identify or fix it.
🧠
23 min
Time to re-enter deep focus after interruption
Once focus is broken, it takes over 23 minutes on average to fully recover. Traditional apps do nothing to prevent this — FocusPulse does.
Process

Three steps to deep work.

From skin to screen — a closed-loop system that monitors, understands, and guides your attention.

01

Biometric Sensing

ESP32-powered wearable sensors capture galvanic skin response, heart rate variability, and motion — the physiological signals of attention.

02

AI Focus Analysis

Our machine learning engine interprets the raw sensor data, classifies your current attention state, and predicts distraction before it peaks.

03

Intelligent Feedback

The mobile app delivers real-time interventions — nudges, break recommendations, and Guardian reactions — timed precisely to your focus curve.
